1. INVITATION

Sealed bids are invited from qualified firms to provide the "DYWA Energy Efficiency and Cooling Project" located in the village of Tafuna, Territory of American Samoa.

2. RECEIPT & OPENING OF BIDS

Sealed bids will be received by the Chief Procurement Officer, American Samoa Government, Tafuna, American Samoa 96799, until 2:00 p.m. (local time), Thursday, September 18, 2025 at which time and place the sealed bids will be publicly opened and read.

3. PRE-BID CONFERENCE

A mandatory Pre-Bid Meeting will be held on Wednesday, September 10, 2025, at 10:00 a.m. at the Office of Procurement Conference Room. The mandatory site visit will immediately follow prebid meeting. Bids will not be accepted from bidders who are not present at the Pre-Bid Conference and site visit.

4. CONTRACT DOCUMENTS

  • Electronic copies of contract documents, including Plans and Specifications may be examined or obtained at the Office of Procurement free of charge or email ilalia.seumalo@op.as.gov for a copy of the IFB.
  • The American Samoa Government reserves the right not to accept the lowest or any bid.
  • The American Samoa Government reserves the right to waive any informality in bidding as may be in the best interest of American Samoa Government.
