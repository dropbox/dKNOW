Prior to accepting a new patient to the facility, the following information must be obtained and forwarded to the clinical team for review:

  1. Patient Face Sheet or Demographics: must include full name, date of birth, social security number, current address and phone number
  2. Proof of Insurance: Copy of insurance cards or face sheet that includes insurance name, type of policy, policy and group number. If Medicare is pending, include letter from social security stating proof of eligibility
  3. Hepatitis B Status. Hepatitis B antigen results <30 days, Hepatitis B antibody/core < 1 year
  4. Labs- results must be in the last 30 days
  5. Proof of Vaccination or declination for Influenza and Pneumonia vaccine
  6. TB Screening: PPD results < 1 year. In the absence of a recent PPD or if the patient has had a positive PPD in the past, copy of Chest X-Ray <30 days
  7. EKG
  8. History & Physical < 1year
  9. Current medications including medication name, dose and frequency
  10. Allergies
  11. Physician Orders
  12. Vascular Access Operative Report
  13. If applicable:

Copy of hospital discharge summary

Copy of last 3 hemodialysis treatment flowsheets

  1. If completed, copy of 2728
  2. Interdisciplinary Assessments and Care Plan
  3. Mode of transportation to dialysis
