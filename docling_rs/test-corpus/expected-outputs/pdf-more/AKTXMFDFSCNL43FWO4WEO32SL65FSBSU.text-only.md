## Twitter workshop at Amity Innovation Incubator

23 August, 2011 Amity Innovation Incubator, sector -125, Noida

A  one  day  workshop  on  using  Twitter  for  business  was  conducted  at  Amity Innovation Incubator by Siddhant Satija, A web 2.0 &amp; Digital Media Evangelist who carries a wide experience over the Internet Domain. The workshop was attended by founders  &amp;  CEOs  of  various  start-ups  that  included  Ambio  Research,  Udaan Ventures,  GVC  Systems,  BiotechNaukri  and  the  Core  Management  team  at  Amity Innovation Incubator.

It started with the basics of twitter and covered aspects like twitter Apps &amp; tools for businesses and how to leverage twitter at various occasions. Various case studies were discussed where  Siddhant explained that with a small network of followers on twitter how he was able to connect with Bollywood celebrities, and people like Vineet Nayar, the  CEO  of  HCL  apart  from  most  of  his  clients  that  he  derives  online  via  twitter. Siddhant is on a mission to educate Indians more about twitter and social networks as he  is  all  set  to  launch  his  new  product  named  '  TwtUncle'  that  is  a  Twitter  based Advertising Application.
