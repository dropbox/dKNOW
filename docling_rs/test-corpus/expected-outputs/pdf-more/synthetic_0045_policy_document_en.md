## Company Policies and Procedures

## Section I: Code of Conduct

## A. Professional Behavior

All employees must maintain professional standards.

## B. Workplace Safety

Safety regulations must be followed at all times.

## Section II: Leave Policies

## A. Vacation Time

Employees accrue 15 days of vacation annually.

## B. Sick Leave

Up to 10 days of sick leave per year.

## Section III: Performance Reviews

## A. Annual Reviews

Conducted each December for all employees.
