Company Overview: Agence Marquetia

Tagline:

MARQUETIA PROVIDES A RANGE OF SERVICES WITH PACKAGES FOR SELLERS AT ALL STAGES OF THEIR AMAZON JOURNEY. WE HAVE SOMETHING TO SUIT ANY BUDGET &amp; WE PROVIDE A RANGE OF SERVICES

Company Description:
Agence Marquetia is a full-service Amazon growth partner providing scalable solutions for sellers at every stage of their journey—from emerging brands to high-volume operations. With flexible service packages tailored to all budgets, the firm functions as a hybrid agency and embedded brand team, delivering measurable results across advertising, operations, analytics, creative, and account growth.

#### Core Offerings

Amazon Division Management:
End-to-end oversight of your Amazon presence, including product listing creation, SEO, operational support, shipping plans, case handling, and A/B testing. Designed to increase traffic, conversions, and profitability while managing logistics for long-term scalable growth. (Up to 5 ASINs). Creatives are not included and require separate quote.

PPC Management:
Strategic campaign builds with a focus on ROAS and ACOS optimization. Includes portfolio creation, keyword targeting, campaign restructuring, competitor and search term analysis, daily optimizations, and complete performance analytics.

Startup Management for New Brands:
Designed for brands with 1–2 ASINs. Combines aggressive PPC strategies, keyword refinement, listing optimizations, and brand protection to generate immediate traction and enable reinvestment for growth.

Analytics &amp; Performance Dashboard:
Automated dashboards deliver real-time insights into key metrics like PPC spend, CTR, ACOS, TACOS, profit, and blended CPA. Clients can view trends by date, SKU, or ASIN—reducing decision-making time and supporting inventory forecasting.

DSP Management:
Enterprise-level targeting using Amazon DSP. Includes onboarding, strategy development, campaign launch, weekly metric reporting, bid adjustments, budget and creative optimization, and custom recommendations tied to your KPIs.

#### Creative Optimization Services

A+ Content Design:
Strategy-led content development for brand-aligned A+ modules. Includes copywriting, design, layout, keyword optimization, and seamless upload to Amazon.

Main Image Revamp:
Analysis of current listings, image retouching, and A/B testing. All imagery is Amazon-compliant and visually optimized to drive click-throughs and conversion.

Brand Story Design:
Narrative-driven branding for storefronts and listings. Includes discovery, storytelling strategy, branded copy, multimedia design, and integration across all customer touchpoints while maintaining Amazon compliance.

Full Page Optimization:
Includes end-to-end listing upgrades—main and lifestyle imagery, A+ content, brand story development, and SEO keyword integration.

Listing Audit + Optimization Scope:
Product category selection, keyword research, image review, copy creation, pricing recommendations, compliance checks, and review strategies to build stronger organic rankings and visibility.
