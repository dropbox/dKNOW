**Compensation Model Ideas**

**Commission Split Structure:**

- Agent or Associate Broker: %
- Qualifying Broker: %
- Sample Realty: %
- Non-company generated leads, split increases.
- Flat fee alternative.

**Qualifying Broker Responsibilities vs Firm Responsibilities:**

- Recruiting and managing agents not required by Broker.

Bonus for Qualifying Broker:

- $ for each new onboarded agent or associate broker.

**Equity Opportunity:**

- Early Qualifying Brokers only.
- % of equity in company.
- Vesting over a few years.
- No cash buy-in.
