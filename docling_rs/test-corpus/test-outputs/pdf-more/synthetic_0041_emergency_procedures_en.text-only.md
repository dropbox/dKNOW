## Office Emergency Procedures

1. Fire Emergency
- Immediate actions
- Activate nearest fire alarm
- Call 911
- Evacuation protocol
- Use designated stairwells
* North stairwell (recommended)
* South stairwell (backup)
- Do NOT use elevators
- Assist those with disabilities
- Assembly points
- Primary: North parking lot
- Secondary: South plaza
2. Medical Emergency
- Call 911 immediately
- Notify building security
- First aid kit locations
- Break room (2nd floor)
- Reception desk
- Gym facility
