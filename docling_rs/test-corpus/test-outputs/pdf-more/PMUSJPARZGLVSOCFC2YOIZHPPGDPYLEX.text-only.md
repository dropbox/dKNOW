## INCLEMENT WEATHER AND EMERGENCY POLICY

- Circuit Court will be closed when the City Government (not Alexandria City Public Schools) is closed or when the City Government is two (2) or more hours late in opening due to inclement weather or emergency.  Refer to the City's website at www.alexandriava.gov or call the City Employee Hotline at 703-746-4636.
- Courts IT Office (policy revised 2/22/2021). The Courts IT office will be open when the City is open or on delayed arrival. Courts IT staff shall follow the City's inclement weather policy and shall report to work if the City is open or on delayed arrival.
